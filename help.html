Help

Login Procedure

To log in to your account, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the Cab Rental Service website.
  2. Click on the
    Login
    button located on the homepage.
  3. Enter your username and password in the provided fields.
  4. Click on the
    Login
    button.

If your login credentials are correct, you will be logged in to your account and redirected to the dashboard.

Registration Procedure

To create a new account, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the Cab Rental Service website.
  2. Click on the
    Sign up
    button located on the homepage.
  3. Fill out the registration form with your personal details, such as username, email, and password.
  4. Click on the
    Sign up
    button.

Once you have successfully registered, you can log in to your account using your chosen username and password.

Cab Request Procedure

To request a cab, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Cab Rental Service account.
  2. Click on the
    Request a Cab
    button on the dashboard.
  3. Fill out the cab request form with the necessary details, including pickup location, drop-off location, date, and time.
  4. Click on the
    Submit
    button.

Once your cab request is submitted, it will be processed, and you will receive a confirmation with the details of your cab booking.
